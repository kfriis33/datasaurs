Where is the data from?

  • We collected two types of data: number of twitter posts about climate change and weight of recycling in four U.S. cities. To get the twitter data, we used a package that scraped tweets that used the keyword climate change. To get the recycling data, we found online datasets for each of the four cities and used pandas to move the datasets into JSONs.
